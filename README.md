# Vexkio / Nekovu Case Study

**AI-powered sales engagement and emotional intelligence infrastructure.**

Vexkio / Nekovu explores a new category of commercial software: sales systems that do not only track what happened, but help teams understand what is happening during customer interactions.

## Problem

Sales teams lose deals because they often cannot read friction, hesitation, intent or emotional shifts in real time. Traditional CRMs are mostly retrospective and rarely help teams perceive the emotional state of a conversation while it is happening.

## Product Direction

Vexkio / Nekovu is designed as a privacy-first sales engagement and emotional intelligence platform with real-time commercial perception, omnichannel workflows, AI copilots, sales playbooks, embedded widgets, mobile direction, CRM evolution through Nexus Force OS and privacy-first affective computing via ALMA Engine.

## Privacy Principle

> The video should never leave the user's device.
>
> The system direction is based on local extraction of numerical signals, minimizing sensitive data exposure and avoiding unnecessary transfer of biometric media.
